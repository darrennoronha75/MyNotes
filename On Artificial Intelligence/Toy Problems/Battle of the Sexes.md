
A coordination game from Lab 4 where players have different preferences but a shared desire to coordinate.

### The Matrix
| | Opera (O) | Football (F) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| **Opera (O)** | $(3, 2)$ | $(0, 0)$ |
| **Football (F)** | $(0, 0)$ | $(2, 3)$ |

*(Note: Payoffs may vary, but the structure is $a > b > 0$)*

### Analysis
* **Conflict**: Player 1 prefers Opera ($3>2$), Player 2 prefers Football ($3>2$).
* **Coordination**: Both prefer being together (payoff $>0$) than apart ($0$).
* **Nash Equilibria**:
    1. **Pure**: $(O, O)$ and $(F, F)$ are both PSNEs.
    2. **Mixed**: A third equilibrium exists where players mix strategies, often resulting in miscoordination (both go to different places).
