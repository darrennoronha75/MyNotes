
A game from Lab 4 representing a conflict between safety and social cooperation.

### The Matrix
| | Stag (S) | Hare (H) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| **Stag (S)** | $(4, 4)$ | $(0, 3)$ |
| **Hare (H)** | $(3, 0)$ | $(3, 3)$ |

### Analysis
* **Scenario**: Two hunters can cooperate to catch a Stag (high payoff) or hunt alone for a Hare (low but safe payoff).
* **Risk**: If one hunts Stag and the other hunts Hare, the Stag hunter gets nothing ($0$).
* **Equilibria**:
    * **Payoff Dominant**: $(S, S)$ is the best outcome for both.
    * **Risk Dominant**: $(H, H)$ is the "safe" equilibrium because it doesn't depend on the other player's trust.
