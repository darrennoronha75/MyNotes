
A pure zero-sum game from Lab 4 with no Pure Strategy Nash Equilibrium.

### The Matrix
| | Heads (H) | Tails (T) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| **Heads (H)** | $(1, -1)$ | $(-1, 1)$ |
| **Tails (T)** | $(-1, 1)$ | $(1, -1)$ |

### Analysis
* **Cyclic Preferences**:
    * P1 wants to match (H,H or T,T).
    * P2 wants to mismatch (H,T or T,H).
* **Equilibrium**: The only Nash Equilibrium is a **Mixed Strategy** where both players randomize $50/50$.
* **Analogy**: This is the simplest form of "predicting the opponent," similar to a penalty kick in soccer.
