A symmetric zero-sum game with no pure strategy Nash Equilibrium.

### The Matrix
| | R | P | S |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **R** | $(0, 0)$ | $(-1, 1)$ | $(1, -1)$ |
| **P** | $(1, -1)$ | $(0, 0)$ | $(-1, 1)$ |
| **S** | $(-1, 1)$ | $(1, -1)$ | $(0, 0)$ |

### Equilibrium Analysis
* **PSNE**: None exists because for any strategy pair, at least one player has an incentive to deviate.
* **MSNE**: The unique Nash Equilibrium is $((\frac{1}{3}, \frac{1}{3}, \frac{1}{3}), (\frac{1}{3}, \frac{1}{3}, \frac{1}{3}))$.
* **Logic**: Using the lemma for [[Mixed Strategy Indifference]], each player must play their strategies such that the opponent is indifferent between Rock, Paper, and Scissors.
