
Relates the value of a game to the maximin strategy.

> [!abstract] **Lemma Statement**
> In any game, the value a player receives in a Nash Equilibrium is at least as high as their **Security Level** (Maximin value).

### Context
* **Maximin Strategy**: A player maximizes their minimum payoff.
* **Security Level**: The value $v_i = \max_{s_i} \min_{s_{-i}} u_i(s_i, s_{-i})$.
* **Significance**: This establishes a "floor" for payoffs in rational play.
