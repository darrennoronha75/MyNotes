
A game modeling conflict over a resource, resulting in different equilibria based on the cost of the fight.
### The Matrix
| | Hawk (H) | Dove (D) |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| **Hawk (H)** | $(\frac{\alpha}{2}, \frac{\alpha}{2})$ | $(\beta, 1-\beta)$ |
| **Dove (D)** | $(1-\beta, \beta)$ | $(\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2})$ |

### Regimes of Equilibrium
The Nash Equilibria depend on the variables $\alpha$ (survival rate) and $\beta$ (benefit of aggression):
1. **Aggressive Regime**: If $\frac{\alpha}{2} > 1-\beta$, then $(H, H)$ is the unique PSNE.
2. **Anti-Coordination Regime**: If $\frac{\alpha}{2} < 1-\beta$, then there are two PSNE: $(H, D)$ and $(D, H)$, plus a Mixed Strategy equilibrium.
