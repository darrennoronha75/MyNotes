
Alethic logic is the logic of "Truth" and "Necessity" in the most general sense.

### Interpretation
* **$\Box \phi$**: Necessarily $\phi$.
* **$\Diamond \phi$**: Possibly $\phi$.

### Validities
Alethic logic is the "strongest" modal logic, typically satisfying **all** the standard axioms (S5 system):
* **T**: If $\phi$ is necessary, it is true.
* **4**: If $\phi$ is necessary, its necessity is also necessary.
* **B**: If $\phi$ is true, it is necessarily possible.
