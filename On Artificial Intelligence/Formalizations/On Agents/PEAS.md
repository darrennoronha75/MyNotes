The PEAS framework is used to formally define the task environment for an agent.

> [!info] **The 4 Components**
> 1. **Performance Measure (P)**: The criterion for success (e.g., "Cleanliness," "Safety," "Speed").
> 2. **Environment (E)**: The external world the agent operates in (e.g., "A room," "Public roads").
> 3. **Actuators (A)**: The mechanisms used to act (e.g., "Wheels," "Gripper," "Display").
> 4. **Sensors (S)**: The mechanisms used to perceive (e.g., "Camera," "Radar," "Keyboard").

### Example: Self-Driving Car
* **P**: Safety, time, comfort, legal compliance.
* **E**: Roads, traffic, pedestrians, weather.
* **A**: Steering, accelerator, brake, signals.
* **S**: Cameras, LiDAR, speedometer, GPS.
